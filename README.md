## Overview
This repository serves as a professional portfolio documenting the completion of the Google Project Management Professional Certificate offered through Coursera.

This program includes over 140 hours of instruction and hundreds of practice-based assessments, simulating real-world project management scenarios. The curriculum was exclusively developed by Google employees with decades of experience in program and project management.

## Technical Proficiencies
- **Project Lifecycle:** Managing projects from initiation to closing using industry-standard methodologies.
- **Planning & Execution:** Developing project charters, RACI charts, and comprehensive project/risk management plans.
- **Methodologies:** Implementing Agile principles and Scrum frameworks to manage iterative project workflows.
- **Data-Informed Decision Making:** Prioritizing tasks based on data, maintaining budgets, and delivering professional stakeholder reports.
- **AI Integration:** Utilizing Generative AI to streamline project documentation, risk identification, communication, and team retrospectives.
